## About the School

Standard Hill S.S Kalungu is a leading secondary school dedicated to nurturing academic and moral excellence. Located in the heart of Kalungu district (along Masaka road), we aim to provide holistic education that empowers learners to excel in academics, sports, and leadership. We are committed to producing responsible citizens who transform communities with self-esteem and teamwork.

## School Mission & Vision

**Motto:** Our utmost is excellence

**Mission:** To provide quality secondary education that develops well-rounded individuals equipped with academic knowledge, moral values, and practical skills for personal and community development.

**Vision:** To be a beacon of educational excellence in Kalungu district, producing graduates who are intellectually sound, morally upright, and capable of contributing meaningfully to society.

## Website Features

This repository contains the official school website built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The website includes:

### Pages
- **🏠 Home** (`index.html`) - Welcome and school overview with performance charts
- **ℹ️ About** (`about.html`) - Detailed information about the school
- **📚 Academics** (`Academics.html`) - Academic programs and curriculum information
- **⚽ Sports** (`Sports.html`) - Sports programs and achievements
- **📝 Register** (`register.html`) - Student registration form

### Technical Stack
- **HTML5** - Semantic markup
- **CSS3** (`styles.css`) - Responsive design and styling
- **JavaScript** (`sths.js`) - Interactive features and animations
- **Chart.js** - Performance visualization
- **FontAwesome 6.5.0** - Icon library for enhanced UI
