Admissions

Join Standard Hill School

We welcome applications from motivated students who are ready to commit to excellence. Here's how to join our school community.

Admission Process

1

Obtain Application Form

Visit the school office to collect an admission form or download it from this website. A small form fee may apply.

2

Submit Required Documents

Complete and return the form with certified copies of all required academic certificates and documents.

3

Placement Assessment

Shortlisted candidates may be invited for an entrance assessment or interview conducted by the school.

4

Receive Admission Letter

Successful candidates receive an official admission letter with reporting date, school fees, and requirements.

5

Pay Fees & Report

Pay the required school fees and report on the specified date with all listed items to officially join the school.

Admission Requirements

Documents required for admission:

  • S.1 Entry: PLE results slip & birth certificate
  • S.5 Entry: UCE results slip & school leaving certificate
  • All: Baptism / naming certificate (if applicable)
  • All: 4 recent passport-size photographs
  • All: Copy of National ID or parent's NIN
  • All: Completed admission application form
  • All: School fees (as per current structure)
