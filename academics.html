Academics

Curriculum & Academic Streams

Standard Hill School offers a comprehensive national curriculum aligned with Uganda National Examinations Board (UNEB) standards, across both Arts and Sciences.

📘

O-Level (S.1 – S.4)

Uganda Certificate of Education. Students study a broad range of subjects culminating in the UCE examinations.

📗

A-Level (S.5 – S.6)

Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education. Students specialize in chosen combination subjects for UACE examinations.

🎨 Arts Stream

Humanities, Languages & Social Sciences

Our Arts programme develops critical thinking, creativity, and communication skills essential for law, education, journalism, social work, and public service.

  • History
  • Geography
  • Literature in English
  • Christian Religious Education
  • Islamic Religious Education
  • Economics
  • Fine Art
  • Music
  • Luganda / Kiswahili
  • Entrepreneurship

🔬 Sciences Stream

STEM, Technology & Natural Sciences

Our Sciences programme prepares students for careers in medicine, engineering, technology, research, and applied sciences through rigorous practical and theoretical training.

  • Mathematics
  • Biology
  • Chemistry
  • Physics
  • Computer Studies
  • Agriculture
  • Technical Drawing
  • Geography
  • Additional Mathematics
  • Entrepreneurship
Assessment & Examinations

How We Assess Learning

📝 Continuous Assessment

Regular tests, assignments, and practicals throughout each term keep students consistently engaged.

📊 Terminal Examinations

End-of-term exams are conducted under formal conditions to prepare students for national examinations.

🎓 National Examinations

UCE (S.4) and UACE (S.6) examinations administered by UNEB. We consistently achieve excellent results.
