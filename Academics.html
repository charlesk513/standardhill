BRIEF BACKGROUND

We started the school in 2019 with 15 students in the fisrt term, the number of students increased gradually untill we had 150 students in third term, among them, we had eight candidates in senior four who managed to score four first grades, three second grades and one third grade. Laying a rocky foundation of the school. the year after, we were interupted but covid 19 pandemic. We managed to pass through the pandemic with all our staff.

Our fees structure is affordable at all levels and students optimise the greatness of the school with all we provide.
