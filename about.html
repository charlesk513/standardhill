Our Story

About Standard Hill School

Standard Hill School is a reputable secondary educational institution located in Kalungu District, Central Uganda. Founded with a passionate commitment to providing quality secondary education, the school has grown steadily to become a respected academic centre that parents and students trust.

We offer both Uganda Certificate of Education (UCE/O-Level) and Uganda Advanced Certificate of Education (UACE/A-Level) programmes, with streams in both Arts and Sciences — giving students the flexibility to pursue their unique academic calling.

Guided by our motto — "Our Utmost is Excellence" — every member of our school community, from management to students, is committed to giving their very best in all endeavours.
